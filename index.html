Welcome to the Warriors Books Website!

This is a website focused on the book series Warriors by Erin Hunter. It is a book series about feral cats who live in the wild in four groups called Clans, guided by their spirit ancestors called Starclan. They have a system of ranks with different names for different ranks (leaders and children get special last parts of their names to distinguish themselves from normal warriors.) They compete and frequently fight over territory and prey. Here you can find bios of several main characters in this series.
