Twigbranch

Origin Rank Prophecy about them
Found abandoned in the forest; one of the members of the lost clan Skyclan Warrior "Embrace what you find in the shadows, for only they can clear the sky."

Twigbranch is a cat who was found abandoned with her sister Violetshine. She tries to find out where she came from.

SPOILER ALERT

She discovers that there is a secret fifth Clan, Skyclan, which was overtaken by Twolegs (humans) a long time ago. But now that the rest of the Clans have moved to an area with no Twolegs, SkyClan is on a journey to return.
